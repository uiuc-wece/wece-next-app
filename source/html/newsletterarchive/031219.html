WECE Lean In: Microaggressions

March 25, 5-6:30pm

ECEB 2015

Come join WECE for our March Lean In as we discuss Microaggressions! Microaggressions are verbal or nonverbal forms of communication that act to target an individual based upon their involvement in a marginalized group. Come to discuss and gain insight on how to respond.
